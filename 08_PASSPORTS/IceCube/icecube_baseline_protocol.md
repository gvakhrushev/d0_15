# IceCube HESE Baseline Protocol

Owner: `D0.Passport.IceCubePhasonDecoherence`.

Cert: `05_CERTS/vp_icecube_hese_baseline_comparison.py`.

Dataset: IceCube HESE 12-year event table, pinned by
`08_PASSPORTS/IceCube/icecube_manifest.json`.

Observable: energy-binned event survival / suppression shape using the HESE
`energy` field. Direction (`ra`, `dec`) and reconstruction topology are
available as secondary stratification fields.

Baseline: a no-decoherence HESE exposure/flux null must be pinned before
physical PASS/FAIL. Acceptable baselines are a published HESE expected energy
distribution, a public no-decoherence effective exposure model, or a declared
conservative empirical null with exposure correction.

D0 prediction: the frozen Hurwitz phason damping curve generated by
`05_CERTS/vp_neutrino_phason_decoherence_passport.py --mode hese12`.

Statistic: compare the energy-binned HESE survival shape against the
no-decoherence baseline and the frozen D0 damping curve. If topology is used,
the track/cascade ratio versus energy is secondary and must not change the
primary event set.

PASS criterion: `PASS_ICECUBE_PHASON_DECOHERENCE_HESE12` is allowed only when
the pinned D0 curve improves the declared statistic against the no-decoherence
baseline without retuning the D0 kernel or event selection.

FAIL criterion: `FAIL_ICECUBE_PHASON_DECOHERENCE_HESE12` is required when the
declared no-decoherence baseline beats or matches the frozen D0 curve under the
predeclared statistic.

SKIP criterion: `SKIP_NEUTRINO_PHASON_DECOHERENCE_BASELINE_REQUIRED` is required
when the HESE event table is present but the no-decoherence exposure/flux
baseline is not pinned.

Forbidden operations: free damping exponent as core; free energy threshold tuned
after HESE; post-hoc topology selection; discarding events after seeing result.
