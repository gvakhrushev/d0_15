| Role | Function in the detector grammar |
|---|---|

```math
ABCD\times\{+,-\}\Rightarrow\Omega_8.
```

```math
\Omega_8+\omega_0=V_9,
```

and the finite scene is oriented by the odd addressed runtime ladder

```math
V_9\rightarrow V_{11}\rightarrow V_{13},
\qquad
```
