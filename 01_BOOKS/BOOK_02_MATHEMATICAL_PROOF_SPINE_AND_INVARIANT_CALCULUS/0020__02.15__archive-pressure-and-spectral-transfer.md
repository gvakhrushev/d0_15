## 02.15 Archive pressure and spectral transfer

The current canonical theorem schema for transfer is:

```math
C_{static}^{D0}\rightarrow L_D\rightarrow \operatorname{Spec}(L_D)\rightarrow S_{DE}(u)\rightarrow\mathcal T_{\mathcal O}^{D0}\rightarrow\mathcal O_{obs}.
```

The BAO/archive-pressure comparison protocol is a strong empirical example, but this book records the general typed rule: the static coefficient, finite spectrum and transfer window must be fixed before external comparison.

**[Finite Feshbach -> profinite tower].** The finite Feshbach-Schur effective operator `W_eff(z)=A-B(D-zI)^-1 C` (depth-2 pole sum = `alpha_alg^-1`, `D0-ALPHA-FESHBACH-RESIDUE-FINITE-SUM-001`) extends to the canonical phi-ladder refinement tower with weights `r^N` (`r=phi^-3`) and `2^11` Boolean-ledger increments (`D0-ALPHA-PROFINITE-SPECTRAL-TOWER-OWNER-001`, Lean `D0.Spectral.AlphaProfiniteSpectralTower`). This is the ADMISSIBLE infinite-pro-object route; the finite heat trace itself is analytic at `0` and carries no `1/s` pole (`D0-DIXMIER-FESHBACH-FINITE-HEATTRACE-001`).
