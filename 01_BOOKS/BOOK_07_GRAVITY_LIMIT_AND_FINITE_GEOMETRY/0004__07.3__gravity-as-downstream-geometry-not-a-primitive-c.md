```math
g_{ij}^{D0}=\operatorname{Cov}_{K_\varphi}(\ell_i,\ell_j).
```

The bridge to continuum geometry is a functorial/macroscopic shadow:

```math
g_{ij}^{D0}\xrightarrow{\mathcal F_{macro}} g_{\mu\nu}.
```
