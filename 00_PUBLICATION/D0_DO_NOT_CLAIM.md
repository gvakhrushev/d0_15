Status: FINAL-PUBLICATION-GUARDRAIL / FORBIDDEN-PROMOTION-LIST

GW170814 proves phi, phi^(5/4), or any fixed alpha law.

## Enforcement

This list is a guardrail, not a theorem. It exists to keep the publication claim hierarchy stable.
